About

How I got here.

I didn't plan on customer success. I started in creative studios, then took a support role at a payments company and found I liked the part of the job most people avoid: staying with a frustrated customer until the problem finally makes sense.

That became the career. At Duve I went from answering tickets to running 70+ enterprise hotels and shipping the AI that now handles most of their guest messages. Today I run a $105M portfolio at Unity, helping studios adopt AI without the hype.

The throughline is simple. I get people to actually use what they bought, and when something is missing, I build it.
